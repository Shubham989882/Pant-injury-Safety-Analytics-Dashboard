🛡️ Plant Injury & Safety Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze workplace safety incidents, injury trends, risk exposure, and operational safety performance across multiple plants.

📊 Dashboard Overview

This project transforms raw safety data into actionable business insights using Excel Dashboarding techniques.

The dashboard helps organizations:

Monitor incident trends Identify high-risk plants and departments Analyze shift-wise safety performance Track injury types and safety costs Improve workplace safety decisions 📌 Key Features

✅ Incident trend analysis ✅ Plant-wise and department-wise monitoring ✅ Injury type distribution ✅ Shift-wise incident comparison ✅ Age-group incident analysis ✅ Safety cost tracking ✅ Risk alerts & recommendations ✅ Interactive slicers and filters

🛠️ Tools Used Microsoft Excel Pivot Tables Pivot Charts Conditional Formatting Data Cleaning Dashboard Design

📈 Business Insights Kolkata plant reported the highest number of incidents. Burn, Slip/Trip, and Fall were major injury contributors. Day shift recorded higher incident counts. Shipping department showed higher operational risk exposure. Employees aged 36–55 were involved in most incidents.

🚨 Risk Alerts High incident concentration in specific departments Increased days lost due to injuries Higher safety cost impact in high-risk plants

✅ Recommended Actions Conduct focused safety training programs Strengthen shift supervision Improve PPE compliance Implement preventive maintenance checks Increase operational safety audits
